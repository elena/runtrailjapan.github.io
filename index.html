The Plan

So, you want to run the toughest 50 mile foot race in Japan? Sure, but we can do better than that!

Train on Course

Leading up to the race, we’ll guide you on several sections of the course. You’ll get used to the terrain and the course, so you can be confident you’re on track during the race.

Meet the Locals

Meet up with local runners will join us on some of our runs. Among them will be other competitors and race volunteers. Energy supplements made from local ingredients will also be available for tasting.

Run the Race

Now that you’ve trained for the race, know the course, and made friends with the competition, all that’s left to is toe the start line with 500 other crazy people.
